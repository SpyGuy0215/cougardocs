SPARK MAX Motor Controller

The SPARK MAX Motor Controller is a motor controller made by REV Robotics. It is a device that can control both brushed and brushless motors, mainly NEOs and NEO 550s.

Connections

  • SPARK MAX Power to PDH

  • SPARK MAX Ground to PDH

  • CAN Wire Ports (G and Y) to CAN Chain
